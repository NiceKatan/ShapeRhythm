ShapeDancer Dataset

ShapeDancer is a multimodal dance generation dataset that contains rich dance sequence information and dancer body shape information, namely body shape text description, which aims to solve the problem of lack of body shape perception in existing music-driven dance datasets.

Each Dance Sequence Includes:

  • Original WAV format music files with totally 10+ music types.
  • SMPL parameters, including 10 shape parameters \(\beta\) and 24 pose parameters \(\theta\) along with the translation.
  • Parameter-aligned text descriptions of the dancer bodyshape.

ShapeRhythm Framework

ShapeRhythm mainly consists of two parts, i.e. FreeShape and FreeDance. FreeShape realizes text-guided freestyle bodyshapes \(\beta\) in SMPL conversion with the textual shape descriptions as inputs. FreeDance is a personalized, harmonic and freestyle dance generation framework via Diffusion Transformer. FreeDance integrates the diverse music inputs and the rendered diverse SMPL bodys, and finally outputs diverse dance sequences.
